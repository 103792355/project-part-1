DS001 — Data Scientist

Design, build, and operate reliable data pipelines and platforms at scale.

  • Job code: DS001
  • Salary: 25–40M VND/month
  • Reports to: Data Engineering Lead

Key responsibilities

  1. Build and maintain scalable batch and streaming data pipelines.
  2. Design and implement ETL/ELT workflows and robust data models.
  3. Ensure data quality, reliability, and performance across systems.
Essential requirements
  • Strong Python and SQL.
  • Orchestration (Airflow/Prefect) and warehouses (BigQuery/Redshift/Snowflake).
  • Cloud experience (AWS/GCP/Azure) and Git/CI.
Preferable
  • Spark or Flink; Kafka or Kinesis.
  • dbt and Terraform/IaC.
  • Observability and data security best practices.

DS002 — Data Scientist (Analytics)

Model and deliver curated analytics datasets and marts for BI and self-service.

  • Job code: DS002
  • Salary: 22–38M VND/month
  • Reports to: Analytics Engineering Lead

Key responsibilities

  1. Design dimensional data models and star schemas.
  2. Implement ELT pipelines into the warehouse and build data marts.
  3. Define metrics and improve data usability with stakeholders.
Essential requirements
  • Expert SQL and warehouse experience.
  • dbt or similar transformation frameworks.
  • Data modeling best practices.
Preferable
  • BI tools (Looker/Tableau/Power BI).
  • Statistics and experimentation knowledge.
  • Data governance and cataloging.

DS003 — Data Scientist (Platform)

Build and operate the data platform, tooling, and infrastructure.

  • Job code: DS003
  • Salary: 28–45M VND/month
  • Reports to: Platform Lead

Key responsibilities

  1. Own orchestration, scheduling, and reliability for pipelines.
  2. Develop CI/CD and infrastructure for data services.
  3. Implement observability and performance optimization.

  • Cloud (AWS/GCP/Azure) and Terraform/IaC.
